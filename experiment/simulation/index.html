Theory

Reynolds Number is a dimensionless quantity:

Re = (ρ × V × D) / μ

Where:

  • Re = Reynolds Number
  • ρ = Fluid Density (kg/m³)
  • V = Flow Velocity (m/s)
  • D = Pipe Diameter (m)
  • μ = Dynamic Viscosity (Pa·s or Ns/m²)
